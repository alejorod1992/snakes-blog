Information

They are reptiles with elongated and narrow bodies that have no limbs. There are currently more than 3,000 different species of snakes, which inhabit all continents except Antarctica. Snakes are carnivorous and feed primarily on mice, lizards, insects, fish, frogs and even eggs to survive. Although snakes are considered pests in some areas, they help control the rodent population.
